About Me







Hey there, my name is "T" (A nickname I go by), and I'm a 26-year-old web design student. I moved to Seattle a little over a year-and-a-half ago from Tampa, Florida.
I basically moved here to escape the terrible heat and giant confederate flags. I currently work as a barista in the Central District, after doing bike shop/mechanic work for five years.
I do cartooning in my free time, as well as plant-keeping, gardening, and cycling.
My goal in life is to have a career with limited human contact and a decent salary. I feel like that's everyones dream, though.

I moved to Seattle two years ago for a change of pace. I find inspiration in everything around me and create one of a kind designs unavailable anywhere else. What inspires me? Life, I like capturing moments, watching performers in the park, morning coffee, farmers markets, and adding color to it.
